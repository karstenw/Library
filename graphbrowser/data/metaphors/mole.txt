Seeing a mole in your dream, represents destruction and unforeseen danger. You are secretly plotting and working against others. symbolizes secret rivalries and hidden agendas.  Dreaming that you catch a mole indicates that you will overcome any adversaries and rise to success.  Dreaming that you have a mole on your face or body indicates that something is interfering with your personal esteem or that you are unable to obtain the esteem of others.
