Seeing triplets in your dream means that you will be met with success in situations where you assumed failure was expected.  For a man to dream that his lover is having triplets means that a menacing dispute will come to a pleasant and amicable end.  For a woman to dream that she is giving birth to triplets, symbolizes that she will succeed in business and wealth, but fail in love.  Hearing or see crying triplets in your dream means a hasty reconciliation to a disagreement.
