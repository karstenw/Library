Seeing or working with clay in your dream, represents creativity and the ability to shape your mind.  It may also mean that you are able to manipulate things to your advantage.  Alternatively, it indicates your need to set some goals and plans for yourself. You have some growing up to do and need to plan for the future. According to Freudian perspective, clay symbolizes feces.  Seeing a clay pot in your dream means devotion, virtue or purity. The clay pot is also a healing symbol.
