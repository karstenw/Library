Dreaming of your own weight, represents your self-worth, self-esteem, influence or power or persuasion. It is also indicative of the burdens you carry in life. Alternatively, it may represent your preoccupation with your physical shape and appearance. The dream may also be a pun for "wait".  Dreaming that you are losing weight indicates that you are no longer being weighed down. It is as if something has been lifted off your shoulders.  Dreaming that you are overweight or that you are gaining weight, suggests that you are feeling over-burdened and pressured.  You may be carrying too many responsibilities.  Dreaming that you are underweight, implies that you need to work harder at something. You may also be way out of your league in some situation.
