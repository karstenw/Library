Dreaming that you are attacked by a reptile, forewarns of a serious problem ahead for you. A friend will betray and slander you.  Dreaming that you are handling a reptile without harm to yourself means that you are letting the ill humor and bitterness of your friends bring you down.  You will eventually succeed in regaining their pleasant relations.  Dreaming that you are bitten by a reptile means that you will lose your lover to a rival.  Dreaming that a dead reptile comes to life, symbolizes that your old problems that you thought were resolved and settled will resurface again.
