Seeing a lamp in your dream, symbolizes guidance, hope, inspiration, enlightenment and reassurance.  Seeing a broken lamp in your dream, suggests that you are shutting out those who are trying to help you. It is also symbolic of  misfortune and the approach of bad luck.  Seeing a lamp explode in your dream, suggests that you will make enemies with whom  used to be your friends.  Seeing a dimly-lit or unlit lamp in your dream means that you are being overwhelmed by emotional issues. You have lost your ability to find your own way.
