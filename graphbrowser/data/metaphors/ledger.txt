Dreaming that you are keeping a ledger indicates that you will find yourself in a complicated situation that you will be left to combat on your own.  If the accounts are the ledger are in good standing, then it indicates that you situation will improve in the end. If the accounts on the ledger are worthless, then it means that you will suffer some losses in your situation.  Dreaming that you misplaced your ledger means that your plans will not go accordingly as a result of your neglect.  Dreaming that there are errors in your ledger indicates that you will be involved in some minor disputes and experience small losses.
