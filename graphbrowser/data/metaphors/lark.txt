Seeing larks flying in your dream, symbolizes high aspirations. If they fall during flight, then it indicates that you will be overcome with despair in the midst of your pleasure and joy.  Hearing larks singing in your dream, foretells of success in business. You will find happiness in a change of environment.  Seeing a wounded or dead lark in your dream indicates sadness and gloom.  Dreaming that you kill a lark means a loss of innocence as a result of giving in to your urges and desires.
