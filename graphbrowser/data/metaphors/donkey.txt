Seeing a donkey in your dream, represents your stubbornness and unyielding personality. You are unwilling to cooperate with others. If the donkey is dead, then it indicates that your party hearty attitude will lead to unrestrained immorality.  Dreaming that you fall off or are thrown from a donkey indicates disharmony in love which will lead to a separation.  Dreaming that you are kicked by a donkey, suggests that you are involved in illicit activities, which will bring your much anxiety and fear that you will be caught.  Dreaming that you are leading a donkey by a halter means your leadership in every situation and your way of convincing people to see things your way.
