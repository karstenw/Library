Dreaming that you are doing your laundry, suggests that you are cleaning up your act or change your image.  You are concerned about how you appear to others.  Dreaming that you are having someone else do your laundry indicates gossip.  Dreaming that you are sorting the laundry indicates that you are trying to understand your own feelings and sorting your attitudes.  Seeing a laundry chute in your dream, suggests that you are not allowing others dictate how your should look or act.  Your mind is your own. Alternatively, it means your exploration of your unconscious and/or negative emotions.
