Seeing a mule in your dream, represents stubbornness. You may be overbearing at time.  Dreaming that you are riding a mule indicates that you are undertaking responsibilities that will give you a lot of anxiety.  Dreaming that you are kicked by a mule, foretells disappointments in love and marriage.  Seeing a dead mule in your dream means broken promises and a decline in social status. It is also an indication of your long sufferings.
