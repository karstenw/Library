Dreaming of your boss represents the bossy or authoritative side of your own personality. Your boss may reveal  self-confidence and the assertive aspect of yourself. It is telling of your issues of control and authority. Alternatively,  to see your boss  may indicate your over-involvement or obsession with your work. Negatively, the boss may symbolize your limitations and lack of freedom/originality.  Dreaming that you are afraid of your boss indicates your fear of authority. You may feel that someone else is running your life or dictating what you can and can not do.  Consider also the relationship you have with your boss. This may provide clues to work-related issues that need to be resolved.
