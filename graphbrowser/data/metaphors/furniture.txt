Seeing furniture in your dream, represents how you feel about yourself and your family.  It refers to your relationships with others and how they fit into your life.  Dreaming that you are moving furniture indicates that you are going out of your way to please others. Also, you may be changing your ways and trying to reevaluate your relationships/attitudes.  Seeing old or worn furniture in your dream, symbolizes outdated attitudes, former relationships, and/or old ways of thinking.
