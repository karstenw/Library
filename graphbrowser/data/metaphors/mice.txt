Seeing mice in your dream, symbolizes domestic problems, business loss, deceit, and insincere friends. It represents minor problems in which you are spending too much time dwelling on.  Dreaming that you kill a mouse means that you will overcome your rivals.  Dreaming that a mouse jumps on you or into your clothing, foretells of scandal in which you will largely figure in.  Dreaming that you or someone is Seeing or eating mice indicates that there is something that has been nagging your conscience and you need to get off your chest.
