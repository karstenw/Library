Water, sky, heaven, cold, distance, masculinity, rest, melancholy. Because it’s associated with the color of the sea and the sky, blue has come to symbolize serenity and infinity. That’s especially true of the more greenish shades of blue, such as aqua and teal. On the other hand, cooler shades of blue can have a tendency to cause feelings of sadness. Blue represents truth, wisdom, heaven, eternity, devotion, tranquility, loyalty and openness
